Our current model is highly precise but lacks sensitivity. While we aren't bothering our legitimate customers (0 False Positives), we are failing to catch 64% of fraudulent activity (9 False Negatives). From a financial risk perspective, we are prioritizing customer experience over fraud prevention. My recommendation for the next sprint is to tune the threshold further or explore SMOTE (Synthetic Minority Over-sampling Technique) to help the model learn more 'fraud' patterns.
